These are your Day 1 combos you'll be using forever. You can go from Rookie to Master almost entirely with these depending on your preexisting knowledge. They'll also serve as the foundation for your drive rush extension combos and meter dump KO combos.

None of these combos have any positional requirements, meaning there are no corner-specific combos here.

All of these combos are of easy to average execution.

## Grounded BnBs

I included "basically everything" that cancels into OD lightning legs as a BnB; if you're an experienced player just picking Chun up you'll likely be a bit more comfortable with confirms, and with the buffs since Season 1 it's not a bad way to get some damage and screen space if you're a new player trying to check drive rushes. She doesn't get a lot off OD legs in terms of oki midscreen, but that'll be covered in Okizeme.

| Combo                                            | Damage | Advantage | Notes                                   |
|:-------------------------------------------------|:-------|:----------|:----------------------------------------|
| 2LK/LP > 2LK/LP xx [2]8LK                        |        | +33       | LK SBK has same advantage as HK SBK     |
| L/M/2HP/4HP xx 236KK                             | Varies | +29       | Bad oki midscreen, good oki in corner   |
| 5MP > 2MP xx [2]8MK/HK                           |        | +34/+33   |                                         |
| 2MK/4MP > DRC 5MP > 4HP xx 214P~LK xx [2]8MK     |        | +34       | Poke into DRC for corner carry + oki    |
| 2MK/4MP > DRC 4HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK x2 > jHP.jHP |        | +46       | Optimal for meter build and super links |
| 2HP/4HP xx 214P~LK xx [2]8MK                     |        | +34       | Used in pressure and in jumpins         |

## Jumpin BnBs

Chun has to make choices about her jumpin buttons: most players default to jHK, but you'll need to use jHP to reach depending on spacing.

Most players tend to not be aware that jHK is hitting someone mid-button and giving extra advantage via Counter Hit or Punish Counter state, and some combos that work with that extra advantage won't if it's just a normal hit. In order to get that non-enhanced advantage, you either have to hit jHK deep or use jHP which requires less timing/depth finesse for the necessary advantage.

| Combo                                            | Damage | Advantage | Notes                                                    |
|:-------------------------------------------------|:-------|:----------|:---------------------------------------------------------|
| jHP/HK > 5MP > 2MP xx [2]8MK/HK                  |        | +33       | If you substitute SBK with OD Legs, you get more damage. |
| jHP/HK > 2HP xx 214P~LK xx [2]8MK                |        | +34       | Builds off the 2HP grounded BnB.                         |
| jHP/HK > 2HP xx [2]8HK                           |        | +33       | "Keep It Stupid Simple" combo.                           |
| jHP/HK > 2HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK x2 > jHP.jHP      |        | +46       | Optimal meterless for meter build and super links.       |

## Punish BnBs

It's best to stick with 2HP and 5HP as punish starters early on because they feed into her best oki.

If you counter DI someone, the 5HK starter combo is going to be your most optimal no matter what.

| Combo                                            | Damage | Advantage | Notes                                                                                |
|:-------------------------------------------------|:-------|:----------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 2HP/4HP xx 214P~MK xx [2]8HK                     |        | +33       | Swapping HK SBK allows for flexible routing & spend.                                 |
| 6HK > 3HP xx 214P~MK xx 22HK                     |        | +37       | Optimal meterless. 3HP accounts for distance from opponent.                          |
| 5HP xx 214P~MK xx [2]8HK                         |        | +33       | Punish whiffed DI. Good for when you can't close distance for more optimal punishes. |
| 5HK xx 214P~MK xx [2]8HK                         |        | +33       | Specifically optimal when counter DI-ing the opponent's DI.                          |
