These are Chun's KO combos, used to get the most out of a punish by dumping all resources (drive and super meter). Whiff punishes and blocked minus moves (sweeps, dps, supers) give a button hit punish counter state, so all starters are implied punish counters.

The majority of KO combos will involve the following drive rush cancel sequence: `2HP/4HP xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK > j2MK > jHP.HP`

Get used to this loop because it's going to be in most of these sequences and there are going to be variations of it.

Even though there will be a lot of similarities, it's important to be aware of any changes in advantage and the damage numbers so you know when one of these will KO.

# Medium poke starter

This section encompasses the routes for converting off poke mediums: 6MP, 2MK, and 2MP.

The route for 6MP and 2MK is:

`6MP/2MK xx 214P~MK xx DRC 4HP xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK > j2MK > jHP.HP (> super)`

# 2HP/5HP starter

The simple 2HP meter dump combo route is:

`2HP xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK > j2MK > jHP.HP (> super)`

This is your easiest and best grounded punish against reversals and supers. There's a version that replaces a drive rush with OD fireball and it does slightly more damage, but we're talking 20 to 30 more damage depending on super.

| Combo | Damage | Advantage | Notes |
|-------|--------|-----------|-------|
| 2HP simple combo > super 1 | 4528 | +26 | In corner: whiff 5MP as a framekill. Press 5MP again to make it hit meaty (+4 OB; +9 OH; +10/11 CH). Allows for throw/shimmy in place of 5MP. |
| 2HP simple combo > super 2 > j.HP | 4858 | +40 | In corner: whiff 5HK as a framekill. Press 5MP again to make it hit meaty (+3 OB; +8 OH; +9/10 CH). Allows for throw/shimmy in place of 5MP. |
| 2HP simple combo > super 3 | 6018 | +6 | Typical super 3 ender. |

5HP as a starter uses the same combos, the enders give the same oki, but sacrifices about 100-ish damage.

# 6HK starter

6HK is a slightly less optimal starter but has slightly different frame data and an extra meterless ender. The route is:

`6HK xx 3P xx 214P~MK xx DRC 4HP xx 214P~MK xx DRC 4HP xx 214P~MK > (ender)`

# jHK/jHP starter

Chun has an optimal jumpin combo involving a mix of drive rush cancel and OD fireball. It varies based on where you are on screen, and like all things fighting games, has a more powerful version when your opponent is cornered.

Midscreen, the route is:

`jHK/HP > 5HK xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~MK xx [4]6PP > 5LK xx DRC 5LP > 4HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK > j2MK > jHP.HP`

In the corner, the route is:

`jHK/HP > 5HK xx 214P~MK xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~MK xx [4]6PP > 5MP > 2MP xx DRC 2HP xx 214P~HK > j2MK > j2MK > jHP.HP`
