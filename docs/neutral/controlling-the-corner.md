# External notes

Sometimes things don't go right or opportunities for advantage present themselves but pursuing them would put you in burnout. 

This is a great clip because Shine dashes once after the safejump setup, framekills with a 5lp and then does it again as his oki button of choice. Since he's in burnout, he keeps things as safe as possible and is willing to give up screen real estate once he's at the end of his pressure, BUT he stands his ground at the point on screen where he backdashed to. Other people tend to just back up and give up a lot of space, but Shine holds closer because he knows if he isn't primed to check a jumpin or dash/drive rush that's ggs.
