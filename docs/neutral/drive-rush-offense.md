# Basics

When trying to do cheap offense with 2MK drive rush cancel, it's sometimes a good idea to lead with 2LK over 2LP because it checks people walking out of pressure, gives you the same +2 on block for strike/throw, but you can opt to chain another 2LK after the initial one to continue checking backwalk or space yourself out from being thrown. A nice mixup within the button choice is that 2LP has shorter blockstun than 2LK, so against more methodical opponents you can switch buttons up a bit and throw off their rhythm.

Additionally, as demonstrated above, you can choose to do something simple like 2LK > 2LK > 5LP > backdash to return to neutral in this situation.

The nerf to 2MK drive rush cancel offense which forces strike/throw as the only offensive options was pretty potent (if you try to shimmy you either get hit or throw), but if you notice your opponent delay techs in these situations, you can still get a shimmy from it.

# Drive rush lows

When doing drive rush 2MK to catch someone walking back or not blocking low, try to make it a habit to cancel it into stance LK. On counterhit, stance LK combos and you get MK spinning bird kick and its oki. An easier but less rewarding conversion is to cancel into stance MP.

On block/regular hit, you can safely cancel stance LK into fireball.
