When in this corner escape situation or when crossing up Zangief (charged) 5HP, her 5HP and 5HK will hit here, so will 4MP. 4MP isn't as rewarding but the combos here are:

- 5HP/HK > stance MK xx [2]8HK
- 4/6MP xx 236HK (> 22HK if this happens in the corner)
