If a Rashid does round start-ish positioning (can be a little out of range) super 2, you can react with OD hazanshu and commit to canceling into super 2 or super 3. Super 2 is a mostly superior option because even though it does 35% with the sbk ender, you get great corner carry with it.

This tech doesn't work if he sets it up after an eagle spike.
