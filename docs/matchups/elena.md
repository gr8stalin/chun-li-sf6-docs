If Elena throws her opponent in the corner, she can drive rush after it and her go-to button is 5mk. She can beat strike, throw, and jump, but she loses to reversal (super, ex dp, drive reversal).

In that situation, her 5MK frametraps into itself. A lot of Elena players like to do the cheeky triple 5mk, but it's only +1 normally and is 6f startup so after the second one you can usually do 5lp to counterhit the third one. Anything else will counterhit trade.

Elena 6HP is a popular round start option because it's easy to space, has long reach, and looks like other normals and specials she has. It's in your favor to do round start small walk back to set up for a 5HP or 2HK whiff punish, but another option to combat this is walkup 5LP since Elena's 6HP is 16f startup and you can technically react to it.

If Elena does drive rush overhead on block into 5MK into 5HK, 2MK will always be in range and hit with punish counter state.

If she drops it and keeps rolling, Elena's 214K special can be crouched on its last hit and punished with 2HP instead of a lesser standing punish with 5MP if blocked. The usual metered routes while not in burnout are good, but when in burnout:

- If you have super 3, do 2HP xx HK legs > super 3
- If you have super 1 or 2, do 2HP > stance HK > j2MK > j2MK > j.HP.HP > super
- If you have no super meter, 2HP > stance MK xx HK sbk

If Elena gets a throw midscreen, she can drive rush 2MK for free regardless of what rise you do. Backrise and blocking it out is safe, backrise perfect parry is a little less safe, but quickrise perfect parry has the most risk but most reward.
