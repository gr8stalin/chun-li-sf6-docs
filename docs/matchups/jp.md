When JP does the MP target combo, you *can* react with perfect parry. It's probably one of the few situations that can be perfect parried by most Master-level players regardless of MR pretty easily.

If you're regular thrown by JP, know that throw followed up by any ghost fireball is a fake follow-up if you commit to drive rush on wakeup. JP is in recovery long enough for even a button like Chun's 5HP to connect into a combo.
