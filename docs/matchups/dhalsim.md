If the dhalsim is shifting into fireball zoning mode, you can chase after them with drive rush. It's not foolproof because he has easy drive rush checks, but drive rush HK hazanshu will go over the fireball. If you catch Dhalsim with the tip of her foot using HK hazanshu, do OD legs since it'll be a punish counter hit.
