# Neutral

## Tiger Knee

Sagat doing tiger knee is an inevitability. It's a good move with high reward and relatively low risk if you don't know the counterplay. Perfect parry is the obvious global answer to things like this but there are some more traditional answers to it.

Chun's jump is floaty but neutral jump works as a punish if you know it's coming, but Chun's backdash covers a lot more distance than average, and 5HP will typically reach whatever was dodged with backdash.

# Me-specific Insights

## Follow up fireball strikes with drive rush occasionally

Chun's regular fireballs aren't plus on hit even when counterhitting normals midscreen. However, most people tend to settle down after getting hit like this, and there's an opportunity to drive rush button following the hit and (re)take advantage.
