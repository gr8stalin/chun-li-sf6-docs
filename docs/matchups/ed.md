# Midscreen

Ed is generally annoying. His ranged buttons are more rewarding on hit/block and have more reach.

His 2HK is annoying because it's easy to space and advances forward quite a bit. If they're telegraphing it, you can walkback whiff punish it, but it's dependent on the Ed player's playstyle and what they know/don't know. If you're too far to whiff punish 2HK but still midscreen, drive rush in after his 2HK whiffs to punish it.

Ed 2MP is also annoying. It doesn't have "reach" but it's a good keep-out tool for Ed because it's very good at clipping walk-up buttons. Thankfully this thing can be whiff punished with just about anything, but it's "impossible" to punish on block as the only thing Chun can really do is challenge with 4MP. You can get a legendary and weird punish on block with 5LK if you're point blank, but the stars have to align for an Ed player to consciously do 2MP that close on block.

Another button to add to the annoying list is 5MK. It's a mid but because it hits so low to the ground there's very little that can actually deal with it. Chun 5HP works if they're just throwing it out there, but a button that actually works because of his extended hurtbox on his body is 5HK. If you stand around the range of his 5MK and it whiffs, her 5HK will hit almost every time depending on range minutia.

Still another is his 2MK. This one is a bit fun because you can crouch under it and get a free 2MK or 2HK punish, but 4MP works just as well. This isn't a button that can be whiff punished with drive rush because it recovers pretty fast (16f).
