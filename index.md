There isn't a lot of great public knowledge about how to play Chun-Li in Street Fighter 6. Already-good players can take her to high ranks purely off their prior experience, and guides have a habit of falling off due to lack of maintenance.

I plan to use this as my personal and public documentation for Chun-Li.

This guide is hosted on GitHub Pages using Jekyll and the "Just The Docs" theme, meaning it is open source. If you would like to contribute, see the [section on contributions]({% link docs/contributing.md %})
